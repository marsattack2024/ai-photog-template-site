# Funnel Design

Product/Service: $ARGUMENTS

## Process
1. Read `docs/market-research.md` for persona and positioning context
2. Read template at `docs/templates/marketing/funnel-architecture.md`
3. Design the funnel architecture
4. Write to `docs/funnel-architecture.md`

## Required Deliverables
- **Entry Point**: Where traffic comes from (paid ads, organic, partnerships, etc.)
- **Lead Magnet**: What the prospect gets for their email. Must be immediately valuable and relevant to the core offer.
- **Core Promise**: One sentence. What transformation does the customer get?
- **Mechanism**: Why this approach works (the "secret sauce" explanation)
- **Offer Stack**: Main offer + 3 bonuses + guarantee. Each bonus must enhance the main offer.
- **Primary CTA**: Exact button text and what happens when they click
- **Conversion Metric**: Target conversion rate with justification

## Verification
- Lead magnet directly relates to the core offer (not a random freebie)
- Offer stack bonuses each solve a specific objection from market research
- CTA is specific ("Start Your Free Trial" not "Submit")
- Conversion metric is a realistic number with reasoning
