# Asset Creation

Product/Service: $ARGUMENTS

## Prerequisites
Read these first:
- `docs/market-research.md` — persona, objections, emotions
- `docs/funnel-architecture.md` — offer, mechanism, CTA

## Deliverables

### 1. Landing Page (`assets/landing-page.md`)
- **5 headline variations** (each targeting different emotion/angle)
- **Subheadline** (expands on the headline with specificity)
- **Mechanism section** (how it works, 3 steps)
- **Social proof placeholders** (what type of proof to add: testimonials, logos, stats)
- **Offer section** (full stack from funnel architecture)
- **FAQ** (5+ questions addressing top objections from research)
- **CTA** (repeated 3x with exact button text)

### 2. Email Sequence (`assets/email-sequence.md`)
7-email nurture sequence:
- Email 1: Welcome + deliver lead magnet
- Email 2: Story / origin (build connection)
- Email 3: Problem agitation (make the pain vivid)
- Email 4: Mechanism reveal (show why this works)
- Email 5: Social proof / case study
- Email 6: Objection handling (top 3 objections)
- Email 7: Offer + urgency + CTA

Each email needs: subject line, preview text, body skeleton, CTA, open loop to next email.

### 3. Ad Copy (`assets/ad-copy.md`)
- **10 hooks** (first lines that stop the scroll)
- **5 angles** (different entry points: pain, aspiration, curiosity, fear, social proof)
- **3 CTA types** (direct, soft, curiosity-based)
- **3 sample ad scripts** (hook → problem → mechanism → CTA, 100-150 words each)

## Verification
- All copy references the specific persona by name/situation (not generic)
- Mechanism is explained consistently across all assets
- Offer matches funnel architecture exactly
- No placeholder text like [INSERT X] or TBD
