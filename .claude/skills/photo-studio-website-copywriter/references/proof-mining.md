# Proof Mining

Use this when the user provides reviews, testimonials, call transcripts, screenshots, or client quotes.

## Proof Categories

Sort proof into these categories:
- Nervous before, happy after.
- Felt comfortable or safe.
- Loved the final artwork.
- Kids cooperated or the session was easier than expected.
- Privacy felt respected.
- Hair, makeup, wardrobe, or styling helped.
- Photographer guided posing.
- Partner or family loved the result.
- Baby safety, patience, or calm.
- Family legacy or emotional reveal.
- Business confidence or branding results.

## Placement Rules

- Hero-adjacent proof: short, emotionally clear quote.
- Problem section proof: quote that names the same hesitation.
- Experience section proof: quote about the process being easier than expected.
- FAQ proof: quote that directly answers the objection.
- Final CTA proof: quote about transformation.
- About page proof: quote about trusting the photographer.

## Editing Rules

- Keep testimonials verbatim unless the user asks for cleaned-up versions.
- Do not invent names, initials, or quote content.
- Do not combine separate reviews into one quote unless labeled as a composite summary.
- Do not use claims like "award-winning" or "top-rated" unless verified.
- Prefer named proof with permission.
- Mark missing proof with specific TODOs.
