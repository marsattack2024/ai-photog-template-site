# Genre-Specific Objections — Complete Library

Each objection is a potential ad angle, quiz question, FAQ entry, and follow-up email topic.

## Boudoir / Portrait

Ranked by frequency:
1. I'm not thin enough — I need to lose weight first
2. I'm too old for this
3. I don't look good in photos
4. What if someone finds the photos (privacy)
5. My partner will think it's silly or won't understand
6. I don't know how to pose
7. I'm not "that kind of woman"
8. I don't own any lingerie
9. My stretch marks / scars / imperfections
10. It's too expensive — I can't justify spending that on myself

**Buyer:** She's the buyer AND the subject. Decision is emotional and personal. The biggest barrier is self-image, not price.

**3 AM thoughts:** "Am I still attractive? When did I stop feeling sexy? Would my husband even notice? I need to lose 20 pounds. I'm not brave enough for this."

## Family

Ranked by frequency:
1. My kids won't cooperate — they hate taking photos
2. No photographer has ever gotten it right with my family
3. What do we all wear — I can't coordinate everyone
4. My husband hates photos and won't participate
5. What if someone's having a meltdown
6. Where do we shoot — I don't know any good locations
7. How long will it take with little kids
8. Are prints included or is everything extra
9. Can grandparents or extended family be in the session
10. We just had photos done [time ago] — is it too soon

**Buyer:** Mom. Decision-maker, planner, outfit coordinator. Dad is along for the ride. She takes 50 photos a day of her kids and is in none of them.

**3 AM thoughts:** "My kids are growing up so fast. We haven't had a family photo since the hospital. I'm never in any pictures. What if something happens and we don't have these?"

## Newborn / Maternity

Ranked by frequency:
1. Is it safe for the baby — what about all those props and poses
2. The baby won't stop crying or won't sleep
3. I'm going to look terrible postpartum — I haven't lost the weight
4. I don't have anything cute for the baby to wear
5. When is the best time to schedule — what if I go into labor early
6. My house is a mess — do I have to host
7. Can siblings be included
8. What do I wear when I'm showing (maternity)
9. Will the photos feel posed and unnatural
10. I just had a baby — I can barely function, how do I plan this

**Buyer:** Pregnant or new mom. Emotional, overwhelmed, nesting. Timeline is biological — newborn window is 5-14 days. Second trimester is ideal booking time for maternity.

**3 AM thoughts:** "Am I ready for this baby? Everything needs to be perfect. I should have booked this weeks ago. Will I ever get this time back?"

## Headshot / Branding

Ranked by frequency:
1. I hate having my photo taken
2. I don't know what to wear — I don't have a "brand" yet
3. Will I look corporate and stiff
4. My team won't take it seriously
5. How fast do I get the images — I need them now
6. Can I get individual and group shots
7. Will retouching make me look fake
8. I don't think I need professional photos (phone is fine)

**Buyer:** Professional. Often employer-funded. Needs images for LinkedIn, website, marketing. Quick turnaround matters.

**3 AM thoughts:** "My LinkedIn photo is from 2019. I look nothing like that anymore. But I hate how I look in photos. Maybe I'll just skip it."

## Pets

Ranked by frequency:
1. Will my dog behave — what if they won't sit still
2. What if my pet is anxious around strangers
3. Do you work with cats or just dogs
4. Where do we shoot — studio or outdoors
5. Can family be in some of the shots
6. How long does the session take

**Buyer:** Emotional. Pet is aging, new puppy, or pet is a family member. Wants to capture the bond.

**3 AM thoughts:** "He's getting older. I can see it. I need to do this before it's too late."

## Wedding / Engagement

Ranked by frequency:
1. How far in advance do we need to book — we just got engaged
2. What if our date changes
3. Do you do both engagement and wedding
4. What's included in the package — what's extra
5. Can we see a full gallery from a real wedding
6. How do we secure our date
7. What happens if it rains
8. How many hours of coverage do we need

**Buyer:** Engaged couple (usually bride is primary decision-maker). Comparison shopping. Has 20 tabs open. Price-sensitive but emotionally invested.

**3 AM thoughts:** "What if we pick the wrong photographer and hate our wedding photos forever?"

## How to Use This List

1. **Ad copy:** Each objection = one Objection-Flipper ad (Stage 4)
2. **Quiz questions:** Top 3-5 objections become quiz questions (Stage 5)
3. **Landing page FAQ:** Top 5 become expandable Q&A section (Stage 5)
4. **Follow-up emails:** Each objection = one email topic (Stage 6)
5. **Phone script:** Objections become discovery questions and objection handlers (Stage 7)
6. **Retargeting carousel:** Each objection = one carousel slide (Stage 4)

The photographer should confirm which objections they hear most often. The ranked order above is based on aggregate data across hundreds of studios — but every market is slightly different.
