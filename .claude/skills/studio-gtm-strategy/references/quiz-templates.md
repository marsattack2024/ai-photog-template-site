# Quiz Inquiry Machine Templates by Genre

## Universal Structure

```
Title Slide → Q1 → S1 → Q2 → S2 → Q3 → S3 → [Q4 → S4 optional] →
Claim Slide (email) → Fair Enough Slide → Name + Phone → [Optional open-ended] → Thank You
```

- 3-5 question/statement pairs (sweet spot: 4)
- Each statement slide: 150+ words minimum
- Each question: one correct answer, 2-3 humorous wrong answers
- Formula: major objection + studio's specific answer = reason to book here

## Boudoir Template

**Title:** "Claim Your $[X] Credit Towards Your Session and Albums! Can You Get 4/4 Right?"

**Q1:** "Do you need modeling experience to have an amazing boudoir session?"
a. Yes, at least 3 years of runway
b. Only if you can do Blue Steel on command
c. No, but 6 months of mirror practice helps
d. Absolutely not — your photographer guides every pose

**Q2:** "What should you bring to wear for your boudoir session?"
a. A full suitcase of lingerie
b. Your most expensive outfit
c. Whatever makes you feel confident — we also have a wardrobe closet
d. Nothing, it's all nude (just kidding)

**S2 (150+ words):** "[Studio-specific wardrobe details — number of pieces, styles, sizes]. You can bring your own favorites too — a partner's shirt, your wedding veil, that outfit that makes you feel unstoppable. We'll help you plan the perfect combination during your pre-session consultation."

**Q3:** "Who will see your boudoir photos?"
a. They're posted on a billboard downtown
b. We share them on social media without asking
c. Only you decide — your privacy is our top priority
d. Your mother-in-law gets a copy automatically

Others choose to share on our social media — and they tell us it was one of the most empowering things they've done."

**Q4:** "When is the 'right time' to book a boudoir session?"

**S1:** "The best time is during pregnancy, ideally in the second trimester. [Studio-specific booking process, capacity limits]. Newborn sessions are best done within the first 5-14 days. By scheduling early, you secure your spot and can relax knowing it's handled."

**Q3:** "What should I bring to my newborn session?"

## Fair Enough Slide (Universal Template)

"Fair enough! Choosing a photographer for [this experience] is an important decision. At [Studio], we believe [USP-driven statement]. [2-3 key differentiators in brief]. If you decide you're ready for [the transformation/experience], we'll be right here waiting. In the meantime, enter your info below and we'll [send you something valuable: a gallery, guide, behind-the-scenes look]."

This slide recovers 10-15% of people who initially said no. Never skip it.

## Implementation Notes

- Platform: Typeform (standard)
- Statement slides: minimum 150 words each
- Photos: 7-10 high-quality portfolio images embedded throughout
- Quiz popup: install via Google Tag Manager for frequency control
- Standalone URL: create for ad traffic and social links
- CRM connection: Typeform native integration or Zapier to GoHighLevel/MeetNikki
- UTM parameters on all quiz links for source attribution
