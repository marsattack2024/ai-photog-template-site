# Copy Templates — Studio Reactivation

All templates are starting points. Customize to the studio's voice, genre, and specific lead context before use. Never send a template verbatim without personalizing the first name and any bracketed fields.

---

## Blast Copy — Text (First Touch)

**Universal (genre-neutral):**
"Hey [Name], it's [Photographer] from [Studio Name]. I'm going through my list and your name came up — I have some openings this [month] and thought of you. Are you still thinking about a session? No pressure, just wanted to check in before I moved on."

**Boudoir:**
"Hey [Name], it's [Photographer] from [Studio Name]. You reached out a while back and I never want to assume the timing is wrong — sometimes life just gets in the way. I have a couple of [month] dates open. Are you still thinking about doing this for yourself?"

**Newborn / Maternity:**
"Hey [Name], it's [Photographer] from [Studio Name]. I know timing is everything with newborn sessions. I have a few spots open in [month] — if your due date is coming up, I'd love to hold one for you. Are you still interested?"

**Family:**
"Hey [Name], it's [Photographer] from [Studio Name]. I'm opening up my [season] calendar and thought of you. Kids don't stay this age long — if you've been thinking about finally getting that family portrait done, now is a great time. Still interested?"

**Branding:**
"Hey [Name], it's [Photographer] from [Studio Name]. I have some [month] branding shoot dates open and thought of you. If you're still looking to refresh your content and headshots, I'd love to make it happen. Still on your radar?"

## Blast Copy — Email

**Subject line bank (5 options — test these, use the one that fits the studio's voice):**
1. I thought of you
2. Still thinking about it?
3. Quick question, [Name]
4. I have an opening this [month]
5. Before I fill this date...

**Email body (universal, customize by genre):**

[Name],

I'm going through my calendar for [month] and your name came up.

You reached out a while back about a [boudoir/portrait/newborn] session, and I never want to assume the timing is wrong — sometimes life just gets in the way.

I have a couple of openings left. If you're still thinking about it, I'd love to make it happen.

Just reply here or text me at [number]. No pressure — I just wanted to reach out before I filled the last spots.

[Photographer name]
[Studio name]
[Phone]

## Maybe Response Copy

### When they reply "I'm still interested but..."

**Text response:**
"Totally get it — life gets busy. I'm actually doing something a little different this month that might make this easier for you. Can I send you the details?"

*Wait for yes, then send event invitation (if running the Group Bonanza) or the Stage 4B sequence.*

### When they reply "Tell me more" or "What are your prices?"

**Text response:**
"Happy to. The easiest thing is a quick 10-minute call — I can walk you through everything and we can see if the timing works. When's a good time for you this week?"

*This re-enters the standard consultation flow.*

## Event Invitation Sequence (Stage 4A — for Maybes)

### Invitation text (sent after maybe response):
"Perfect timing actually — I'm doing something a little different this month. I'm opening the studio for a private preview night on [date] at [time]. You'd get to watch a real session happen, see the images on the big screen, and see the finished products — all before you decide anything. Keeping it to [15] women. Want me to save you a seat?"

### Invitation email (sent same day as invitation text):

Subject: Come see it before you decide

[Name],

I know booking a [boudoir] session is a big decision. You've been thinking about it for a while, and I get that.

So instead of asking you to decide over the phone, I'm doing something different.

On [date] at [time], I'm opening the studio for a private preview night. [15] women, drinks, a live shoot — you'll watch an actual session happen start to finish, see the images revealed on the big screen, and hold the finished products.

No commitment to book. Just come see what it actually looks like.

If you decide it's for you, I have something special for anyone who's ready that night.

[15] seats. Only [X] left.

Want one?

[Photographer name]

[RSVP LINK]

### RSVP confirmation text:
"You're in! Studio Preview Night is confirmed for [date] at [time]. Address: [address]. No prep needed — just come as you are. See you then."

### 48-hour reminder text:
"Hey [Name], quick reminder — Studio Preview Night is [day] at [time]. We're keeping it small, so if anything changes, just let me know so I can offer your seat. Really looking forward to it."

### Day-of text:
"Today's the day! Doors open at [time]. Drinks are ready. Can't wait to show you what we do here. See you tonight."

**Text:**
"Totally get it. Life has its own timeline. I'll check back in a few months — and if you need anything before then, I'm always here."
