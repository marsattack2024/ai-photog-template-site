# QA Checklist

Run this before delivering any Google Ads assets.

## Strategy QA

- One campaign per genre or intent cluster.
- Keywords match one clear buyer intent.
- Landing page matches the campaign genre.
- Offer and CTA match the landing page.
- Revenue Formula lever is clear: leads, booking rate, or average sale.

## RSA QA

- 15 headlines are provided.
- Every headline is 30 characters or fewer.
- At least 3 headlines include the primary keyword or close variant.
- Location appears in relevant headlines.
- 4 descriptions are provided.
- Every description is 90 characters or fewer.
- Headlines are varied, not repetitive.
- Descriptions each do a different job.
- DKI is used sparingly, if at all.
- Location insertion is used only in headlines.
- Pinning notes are included only when necessary.

## Extension QA

- 6 callouts are provided.
- Every callout is 25 characters or fewer.
- Structured snippets include at least 3 values per header.
- 8 sitelinks are provided.
- Sitelink description lines are 35 characters or fewer.
- Promotion extension is included only when a real offer exists.
- Image extension direction matches the genre.

## Proof and Compliance QA

- No invented awards.
- No invented review counts.
- No invented years in business.
- No "#1" or "best" claims without proof.
- No guarantees unless the studio actually offers them.
- No pricing on website or landing page copy unless explicitly approved.
- Boudoir language is tasteful and policy-conscious.
- Newborn safety claims are verified.

## Landing Page QA

- Hero headline matches search intent.
- CTA above the fold.
- Relevant testimonials or TODO proof placeholders included.
- Form is short and mobile-friendly.
- Thank-you page or event tracking exists.
- Page loads on mobile.
- Offer terms are consistent.

## Tracking QA

- Google Ads conversion action defined.
- GA4/GTM event recommended.
- Unique thank-you page recommended.
- CRM sync tested or flagged.
- UTM parameters included.
- Test lead submitted before launch.
