## Copy Customization Rules

1. All copy uses the studio photographer's first name, not "we" — boudoir and portrait are personal businesses
2. Never use exclamation points in closing or follow-up copy — it reads as desperation
3. The word "discount" never appears — always "credit," "waived," or "complimentary"
4. Texts are always short — under 3 sentences for invites, under 2 for reminders
5. Emails are slightly longer but never more than 150 words in the body
6. Subject lines are plain — no emojis, no ALL CAPS, no "LAST CHANCE"
7. Every piece of copy connects back to the transformation, not the mechanics
